# Persistent Storage - User Guide\n\n## ✅ Your Progress is Safe\n\nAll your progress in Cybersec Arena is automatically saved and will NEVER be lost.\n\n## What Gets Saved?\n\n✅ All challenges you've solved\n✅ All badges you've earned\n✅ Quiz scores and statistics\n✅ Firewall game best score\n✅ Your settings and preferences\n✅ Everything else\n\n## When is it Saved?\n\nAutomatically saved:\n- ✅ When you solve a challenge\n- ✅ When you unlock a badge\n- ✅ When you complete a quiz\n- ✅ When you set your difficulty level\n- ✅ When you do anything in the game\n\n**No manual save button needed!**\n\n## When Will I Lose Progress?\n\n**You won't!** Progress persists even when:\n- ✅ You refresh the page (F5)\n- ✅ You logout from your account\n- ✅ Your browser crashes\n- ✅ Your internet goes down\n- ✅ You switch to a different device\n- ✅ You close the browser\n\n## How Does It Work?\n\nYour progress is saved in **TWO places**:\n\n1. **Browser Storage** (Instant)\n   - Saves immediately when you play\n   - Always available, even after logout\n   - Works offline\n\n2. **Cloud Database** (Cloud Backup)\n   - Synced when you're logged in\n   - Available across all your devices\n   - Backup for your progress\n\n## Cross-Device Sync\n\nYour progress syncs across devices:\n\n1. Play on your phone\n2. Progress saved to cloud\n3. Switch to your laptop\n4. Login with same account\n5. See all your progress from phone\n\n**Everything syncs automatically!**\n\n## What If Internet Goes Down?\n\n**No problem!** You can keep playing:\n- Challenges still work\n- Progress still saves\n- Just to browser storage (for now)\n- When internet returns, progress syncs to cloud\n\n**No progress lost!**\n\n## What If I Logout?\n\n**Your progress is still safe!**\n- Logout doesn't delete progress\n- Progress stays on your device\n- When you login again, it's still there\n- Syncs with cloud\n\n## Resetting Your Progress\n\nIf you want to reset:\n\n1. Go to **Profile** (top right)\n2. Go to **Settings** tab\n3. Find **Reset Progress** section\n4. Choose which section to reset\n   - CTF Challenges\n   - Phish Hunt\n   - Code & Secure\n   - Cyber Quiz\n   - Firewall Defender\n   - Or reset all\n5. Confirm reset\n\n**Your choice - we don't force resets!**\n\n## Checking Your Progress\n\n### On your device:\n1. Open browser DevTools (F12)\n2. Go to **Application** tab\n3. Find **Local Storage** in left menu\n4. Look for your domain\n5. Find key: `cybersec_arena_progress_v1`\n\n**You'll see your exact progress data!**\n\n## Troubleshooting\n\n### \"My progress disappeared!\"\n1. Try refreshing (F5)\n2.
